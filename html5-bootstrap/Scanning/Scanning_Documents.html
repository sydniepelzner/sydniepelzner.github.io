Scanning Documents

Follow these steps when scanning a physical document.

The easiest way to access scans is to send it to your email in your desired file form. The following steps detail how to complete this process. For alternate methods, refer to Getting Extra Assistance
  1. Note: Your session will time out every three minutes. For this reason, if you are scanning a large number of documents, divide them into smaller groups and use multiple sessions to scan and send them within the time limit.
    Lift the top of the printer to reveal the scanner and place your document on the clear screen.
  2. Align the top left corner of your document with the red arrow.
  3. Close the top of the printer.
  4. Swipe your card with the black bar facing up through the slot on the right end of the console.
  5. When the console displays 'Select use:', press F2. The corresponding label will say 'Copy'; ignore it.
    The touchscreen on the printer should activate like so: If the screen displays a different image, press MAIN MENU.
  6. Tip: When tapping icons on the touchscreen, press firmly and hold your finger on the screen until the icon responds.
    Tap SCAN AND SEND.
  7. Specify the destination of your document by tapping NEW DESTINATION.
    If this is your first time using the scanning function, your contact information will not appear in the Address Book or as an option for the One-Touch destination.
  8. Tap EMAIL.
  9. Enter the email address you want the scan to be sent to.
    The next screen should look like this:
  10. Confirm that your email is highlighted in yellow under 'Destinations.'
  11. Choose your desired settings, including file type, DPI, and the color scheme (if applicable).
  12. When ready, press the green circular START button on the right side of the touchscreen.
  13. Once the touchscreen reads 100%, tap 'START SENDING.'
  14. When completed, the touchscreen will switch to the Main Menu. Check your email to confirm the successful transfer of your document.
  15. Press F3 on the Equitrac console to complete your session.
Your scan is now in your inbox in your chosen file type. Remove the original document from the scanner.
