Getting Extra Assistance

Need extra help? Contact the following library and technical departments for further questions or clarification.

General Help

  • The Circulation Desk

    • Located on the right side of the first floor.

    • Employees may be able to give general information on the printers; however, note that any specific troubleshooting questions should be directed to the Repro Graphics department.

Multi-Function Printer Help

  • Repro Graphics Department:

    • Repro Graphics owns the multi-function printers, and should be contacted for troubleshooting and machine malfunctions.

      • Email: copierhelp@ucdavis.edu

      • Phone Number: (530) 754-2127

Network Help

If you are having trouble connecting wirelessly, contact UC Davis IT.

  • Email: ithelp@ucdavis.edu

  • Website: https://ucdavisit.service-now.com/servicehub
